# Mockup prompt set

All ten images were generated with the built-in image-generation workflow. No
Saltwind image, texture, or screenshot was used as an input.

## Shared style block

> Quiet geometric modernism; controlled low-poly forms; clean flat-shaded
> facets with soft ambient light; sparse premium materials; crisp silhouettes;
> slightly posterized atmospheric gradients; restrained maritime editorial
> palette; intentional contemporary game art; not photorealistic, not childish,
> not toy-like, no outlines, no generic mobile-game gloss.

## Shared water block

> Standard low-poly water: a single coherent moderately tessellated triangular
> surface with gentle rolling vertex displacement, flat-shaded triangular face
> normals, subtle neighboring blue variation, restrained Fresnel and sun
> highlights, and localized angular wake foam. Never a tiled mosaic, paper
> ribbons, unrelated polygon patches, or mirror-like photoreal water.

## Shared product constraints

> One correctly proportioned modern 6-meter training keelboat, one mast, and
> exactly one mainsail unless the view does not show the rig. Minimal accessible
> interface, exact supplied text only, no Saltwind branding, no logos, no
> watermark, no race gates, no boost, no fantasy elements.

## Screen briefs

### 01 · Launch screen

Open lake at dawn, faint simplified mountain silhouettes in layered haze, one
anchored boat small against the water, wide menu space. Text: “SAILING
SIMULATOR”, “LEARN THE WIND”, “Start sailing”, “Free sail”, “Lessons”.

### 02 · Core gameplay

Behind-and-above chase camera; beam reach; filled cream sail; boom halfway out;
red/blue telltales streaming; modest heel and coherent wake. Text: “Beam reach”,
“5.8 kn”, “Trim: good”, “W / S Sail”, “A / D Helm”.

### 03 · Luffing lesson

Close rear-quarter view; boat too close to wind; angular leading-edge flutter;
wandering telltales; nearly upright, slowing. Text: “THE SAIL IS LUFFING”, “Bear
away or sheet in”, “1.2 kn”, “No-go zone”.

### 04 · Optimal trim

Side-quarter close reach; stable faceted camber; parallel telltales; efficient
wake; controlled heel. Text: “AIRFLOW ATTACHED”, “Hold this trim”, “6.4 kn”,
“Optimal”.

### 05 · Tack lesson

Elevated chase view; one mainsail crossing the centerline; 40° no-go compass;
thin curved path; visible speed loss. Text: “TACK THROUGH THE WIND”, “Keep
turning”, “Speed will fall in the no-go zone”, “3.1 kn”.

### 06 · Gust response

Low rear-quarter view; darker coherent gust band; sail load and heel increase;
skipper eases; wake strengthens. Text: “GUST IN 3s”, “Ease a little”, “Wind 14
kn”, “Heel 18°”.

### 07 · Open-water exploration

Very wide blue-hour view with no shore; boat under 5% of frame; warm navigation
light; immense negative space. Text: “FREE SAIL”, “Dawn · steady breeze”, “7.4 km
from launch”.

### 08 · Shore discovery

Quiet misty cove, simplified rock, sparse pines, small boathouse and navigation
marker; open lake still visible behind. Text: “NORTH COVE”, “Shore discovered”,
“Depth 8 m”, “Slow water”.

### 09 · Cockpit view

Eye-level view from the helm; tiller, sheet, mast pennant, boom, telltales, and
horizon all readable; no dashboard wall. Text: “Close reach”, “Sheet 36%”, “Wind
11 kn”, “Telltales streaming”.

### 10 · Mobile controls

Portrait gameplay view; same art style; spring-centered horizontal helm pad at
lower left; persistent vertical sheet slider at lower right; compact wind and
speed at top. Text: “HELM”, “SHEET”, “Ease”, “In”, “Beam reach”, “5.2 kn”.
